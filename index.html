According to the global hunger index India ranks the 101 position; standing firm with 27.5% of country's population is hungry at the real time. The Hunger Legions is an initiative of a dedicated group of people working together to yield food for the vulnerable people.
Hunger Legions, the warriors for hunger- The warriors who work for filling up empty stomachs, irrespective of their pockets. Eating is a basicity, and food should be accessible to every individual. We have always tried to beyond that supplying love, smiles and satisfiability in small packets of food.
We navigate the extra food in an area and make sure it is'nt wasted and is going in a stomach rather than a drain.
We've tied up with many restaurants and food hubs, which are helping us in this revolution we're trying to initiate and no soul is sleeping hungry
